1. What the app does

Migraine Forecast is a personal health tracking app that helps you identify patterns and forecast risk for migraine attacks. All data processing happens locally on your device.

3. Oura Ring integration

If you choose to connect your Oura Ring account, the app uses Oura's OAuth 2.0 authorization flow to obtain permission to read your Oura data. Specifically:

  • You authorize the app through Oura's own login page. The developer never sees your Oura credentials.

  • The access token, refresh token, and token expiry are stored in secure storage on your device and used solely to fetch your Oura data on your behalf.
  • You can revoke the app's access at any time from the app's Settings screen ("Disconnect Oura") or from your Oura account settings at cloud.ouraring.com.
